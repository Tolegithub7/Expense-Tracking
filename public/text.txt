Expense-Tracking — Expanded Project Specification (for non-technical and technical readers)

1. Executive summary
This is an easy-to-use expense tracking application that helps individuals and small businesses record, categorize, analyze, and export spending. Emphasis on fast entry, clear visual summaries, secure storage, and simple export options.

2. Target users / personas
- Freelancer: tracks client expenses, reimbursable costs, and tax-deductible items.
- Household manager: monitors shared household spending and monthly budgets.
- Student: records daily small purchases and saving goals.
- Small business owner: tracks expenses by project, vendor, and for bookkeeping.

3. Key capabilities (high-level)
- Quick expense entry (amount, date, category).
- Rich metadata: merchant, payment method, receipt image, tags, project.
- Category & subcategory hierarchy.
- Budget creation and alerts.
- Reports: category totals, trends, tax reports, project summaries.
- Export: CSV, JSON, PDF; integrations with accounting tools.
- Multi-device sync and user account controls (future).

4. Detailed features
4.1 Expense entry fields
- id (UUID)
- user_id
- timestamp (ISO 8601)
- amount (decimal)
- currency (ISO 4217)
- date (YYYY-MM-DD)
- category (e.g., Groceries, Transport, Rent, Entertainment)
- subcategory (e.g., Groceries > Produce)
- merchant
- payment_method (Cash, Debit, Credit, Bank Transfer, Mobile)
- tags (array)
- receipt_url (link to stored receipt image)
- notes (text)
- reimbursable (boolean)
- tax_deductible (boolean)
- project (optional)
- location (city, country)
- created_at, updated_at

4.2 Categories and rules
- Support nested categories and user-defined categories.
- Default categories: Groceries, Transport, Housing, Utilities, Entertainment, Healthcare, Education, Income (negative), Miscellaneous.
- Rules: automatic categorization via merchant, amount thresholds, or keywords in notes.

4.3 Budgets & alerts
- Budget per category or overall monthly budget.
- Alert thresholds: 50%, 75%, 90%, and custom.
- Recurring budgets and rollover options.

4.4 Reports & dashboards
- Dashboard widgets: monthly total, category breakdown, 30/90/365 day trends.
- Reports: monthly summary, tax-deductible summary, reimbursable items, project expenses.
- Exportable charts and CSV/PDF reports.

4.5 Search & filters
- Full-text search across merchant, notes, tags.
- Filters: date range, category, payment method, amount range, project, tags.

4.6 Security & privacy
- User authentication (email/password, OAuth).
- Encrypted storage for personally identifiable data.
- Role-based access for shared accounts.
- Export and delete data options.
- Privacy policy: data not shared without explicit user consent.

4.7 Integrations & imports
- Import from bank CSV or standard formats.
- Export to CSV/JSON, connect to accounting integrations (e.g., QuickBooks, Xero) — planned.
- Receipt OCR (planned).

6. UX flow (simple)
- Launch app → Quick add (amount + category) → optional details (merchant, notes, receipt) → Save.
- Dashboard shows daily and monthly totals with quick filters.
- Create budgets and set alerts.
- Export or share reports.

7. Admin & settings
- User profile, currency, timezone.
- Category management and import rules.
- Data export & delete account.
- Backup and restore.

8. Reporting metrics (examples)
- Monthly total spend, average daily spend.
- Category share (%) and month-over-month change.
- Top merchants and recurring payments.
- Tax-deductible and reimbursable summaries.

9. Example KPIs for success
- Time to add an expense: < 10 seconds for quick-add.
- Daily active users for personal plan.
- Percentage of expenses with receipts attached.
- Export completion rate.

10. Roadmap (near-term)
- OCR receipt scanning and auto-fill fields.
- Bank import connectors and reconciliation.
- Multi-currency support with conversion history.
- Shared household accounts and role management.

11. Support & documentation
- In-app help, FAQ, contact email.
- Step-by-step guides: adding expenses, creating budgets, exporting data.
- Data privacy and deletion procedures documented.
